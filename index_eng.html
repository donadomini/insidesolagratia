Reformed Theology

Soli Deo Gloria ! Aut quis prior dedit illi et retribuetur ei, Quoniam ex ipso, et per ipsum, et in ipso sunt omnia: ipsi gloria in saecula. Amen. (Rom 11:35-56)
Glory only to God ! Or who hath first given to him, and it shall be recompensed unto him again? For of him, and through him, and to him, are all things: to whom be glory for ever. Amen.
